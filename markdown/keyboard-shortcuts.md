Jumping around Raycast shouldn’t be difficult. Getting from A to B should be easy, and from B to A should be just as simple. We’ve designed Raycast in a way to make things easier for you, and speed up your process.

The following shortcuts will work anywhere within Raycast

-   `esc` will take you back to the previous page
-   `⌘ esc` will take you back to the main search page
-   `⌘ W` will close the Raycast window
-   `⌘ ,` will open Raycast preferences
-   `⌃ N` or `⌃ P` can be used to move down or up in any list view

Most Raycast windows will have an action panel with actions associated to a list item or a detail view. The following are some common shortcuts associated with list items in the root search.

-   `⌘ ⇧ ,` to open the selected item in Preferences
-   `⌘ ⌥ ,` to open the group of the selected item in Preferences
-   `⌘ ⇧ F` to add or remove an item favorites
-   `⌘ ⌥ ↑` and `⌘ ⌥ ↓` to move a favorite item up and down the list

You will find all the above actions in the Action Panel.
