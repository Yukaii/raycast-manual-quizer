Auto Quit helps you stay focused by automatically closing applications after periods of inactivity. It's designed to minimize distractions and keep you in your flow state by removing apps you don't actively need.

## Features

-   Automatically terminates inactive applications after 3 minutes
-   Configurable per application
-   Ideal for managing distracting apps like chat, calendar, and social media
